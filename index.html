Biography

Dr. [cite_start]Megh Raj is a distinguished Assistant Professor (Senior Scale) at the Faculty of Law, University of Delhi, with over seven years of dedicated teaching experience[cite: 1]. [cite_start]He holds a Ph.D. in Environmental and Natural Resources Law from the same institution, where he also completed his LL.M. and LL.B. degrees[cite: 1]. [cite_start]His expertise spans a wide range of critical legal fields, including Environmental Law, Cyber Laws, Public Interest Law, Competition Law, and Company Law[cite: 1].

[cite_start]

Before dedicating himself to academia, Dr. Raj served as an Assistant District Attorney in the Prosecution Department of the Government of Haryana[cite: 1]. This practical experience has endowed him with a unique and grounded perspective, enriching his teaching and research. [cite_start]An active contributor to academic governance, he is a Member of the Academic Council of the University of Delhi, where he plays a crucial role in shaping curriculum and institutional policy[cite: 1].
