Biography

Dr. [cite_start]Megh Raj has been an Assistant Professor at the Faculty of Law, University of Delhi, since February 2018[cite: 38]. [cite_start]He holds a Ph.D. in Environmental and Natural Resources Law from the same institution[cite: 24]. [cite_start]Before his academic career, he served as an Assistant District Attorney for the Government of Haryana[cite: 36, 40]. [cite_start]Currently, he is an active Member of the Academic Council at the University of Delhi, where he contributes to curriculum development and academic policy-making[cite: 54, 58].
