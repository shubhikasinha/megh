Biography

Dr. Megh Raj has been an Assistant Professor (Senior Scale) at the Faculty of Law, University of Delhi, since February 2018. He holds a Ph.D. in Environmental and Natural Resources Law from the same institution. Before his academic career, he served as an Assistant District Attorney for the Government of Haryana. Currently, he is an active Member of the Academic Council at the University of Delhi, where he contributes to curriculum development and academic policy-making. His research is centered on pressing environmental challenges, cyber regulations, and sustainable development.
